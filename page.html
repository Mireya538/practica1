Welcome!

We are a small professional rescue team dedicated to the care and rescue of cats and kittens. At Kitten Karers we are desperate for loving homes for kittens and young and adults cats in our care.
With a lot of hard work, we were lucky to start our shelter and have been able to successfully keep our doors open for well over 6 years now. It's taken a lot of love, hard work and dedication, but the cats and kittens make every day a joy for us!
